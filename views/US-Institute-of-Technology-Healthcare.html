About USITH

And Spino Inc , the company started providing executive search services, specializing in the placement of computer and financial professionals in the New jersey , New York and All over the USA

We also go beyond training with Workforce Optimization Solutions – a modern approach that improves the adoption of skills, and accelerates the implementation of technical and business processes required to improve IT service delivery. To support both business and government organizations in their workforce optimization efforts,we develop structured learning paths prior to training, and provide implementation servic that extend the value of training long after a training event has concluded.
